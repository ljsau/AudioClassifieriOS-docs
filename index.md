# Audio Classifier

**Know what your world sounds like.**

Audio Classifier turns your iPhone into a round-the-clock sound monitor. Pick the sounds you care about — a barking dog, birdsong, music, a running engine — and it listens, classifies, and charts everything it hears, entirely on your device.

- **300+ sounds** recognised with on-device machine learning — no internet required
- **Live monitoring** with real-time confidence meters and event alerts on screen
- **Built for all-night sessions** — survives calls, alarms, and Bluetooth swaps, and says so plainly when iOS pauses the mic
- **Dashboard & timeline** — pinch, zoom, and scroll through your day's soundscape
- **Private by design** — audio is analysed in memory and never recorded, stored, or uploaded

*Coming soon to the App Store. Also available for Android.*
